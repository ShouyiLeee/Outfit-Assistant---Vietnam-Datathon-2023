Privacy Policy for LooksMatch

At LooksMatch, we prioritize the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how it is used, and the measures we take to safeguard your information when you use our virtual fitting room services.

When you interact with our virtual fitting room, we may collect certain personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Contact information (such as name, email address)
  • Measurements and body dimensions for the purpose of providing personalized fitting recommendations

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To process orders, fulfill requests, and deliver products or services
  • To communicate with you regarding your account, orders, and updates about our services
  • To enhance and improve our virtual fitting room technology and user experience
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activities and ensure the security of our website and users' information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
